Aircraft Health Information

TYPE Inspection Accomplished: Preflight (PR), (PR/BPO), Thruflight (TH)
ACCOMPLISHED BY Minimum Signature - First initial last name Ex. Jdoe
COMPLETED DATE Enter date - Formatted as YYYYMMDD
TIME Time inspection completed - Formatted in 24 hour clock and on Zulu time.
STATUS TODAY Enter the highest discrepancy in the 781A and K.
Red X- Most serious condition.
Dash- Less serious than X, more serious than Diagonal.
Diagonal - Least serious condition.
BOX NO Used to record the box number of the STATUS TODAY column for an exceptional/conditional release is being signed.
EXCEPTIONAL RELEASE Required before flight. Will not be signed if there is a current X.
AIRFRAME TIME Upon initiation of a new form, transcribe the total time from the TOTAL block of the previous form to the PREVIOUS block of the new form.

Ensure airframe time is updated in the Form 781 J.
LANDINGS Upon initiation of a new form, transcribe the previous FULL STOP, TOTAL and LANDING GEAR CYCLES.

Aircrew will document the applicable criteria.
CART/JFS STARTS Aircrew will document this as applicable.
ENGINE CYCLE DOCUMENTATION: Aircrew will document this as applicable.
SERVICING DATA Encompasses Fuel, Oil, Oxy, Nitrogen and Water.
OCTANE or GRADE Enter the fuel grade/octane that was serviced.
Ex: JP-8 or JET A
QTY SRVCD Enter total quantity of fuel serviced or drained at on operation, followed by P (pounds) or G (gallons).
TOTAL IN TANKS Upon completion of servicing/draining/in tank check, enter the total number of P or G of fuel onboard in all tanks.
OIL Line out all but applicable words from the title of this column.
Ex: Half pints, pints, quarts, gallons or liters

In the SER column, enter the number of units serviced. Then record the total number of oil in each tank.

If no servicing was conducted, enter a black dash ( - )
If no servicing was required during checks, enter a zero ( 0 )
OXY PRESS OR QTY Line out the non-applicable portion of the title.
Enter the pressure/quantity serviced

If no check is required, enter a dash.
