      • 29-00 - Genral
        • ♦ Special Maintenance Requirements (29-00-00)

The time in this section could not be calculated, because the time range is too large. The range of time can be from 1 minute to 24 hours.

Seal leakage types are as follows:
D = Dynamic leakage
S = Static leakage
S-D = Static leakage thru dynamic seal
MTG FACE = Mating surface face seal leakage.
Components in unpressurized condition, as in a parked aircraft with the hydraulic systems turned off, are allowed a maximum leakage of 2 drops per seal per day.

20 drops equals 1 milliliter.

No leakage allowed for Lee plugs.

No leakage allowed for Rosan fittings.

No leakage for flareless tube fittings, radial swaged, axial swages, and cryogenic shape memory fittings, except for a slight wetting at fittings, insufficient to form a drop in 10 minutes, at operating pressure.
